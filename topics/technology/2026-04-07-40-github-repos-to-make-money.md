## Summary

A curated thread by @oooodjdjd (posted Apr 5, 2026, 155.6K views) listing 40 high-quality GitHub repositories (average 20k+ stars) organized into 5 monetization themes. Aimed at developers looking to build solo businesses, invest algorithmically, automate content creation, or find remote work.

## Key Takeaways

- All 40 repos average 20k+ stars and are actively maintained
- Covers the full spectrum from building products to investing to content creation
- Strong China/overseas indie developer perspective — several repos specifically address going global (出海)
- Quant trading section is notably comprehensive, covering stocks, crypto, and prediction markets
- Several AI-native tools (multi-agent trading, AI video generation) represent the newest wave
